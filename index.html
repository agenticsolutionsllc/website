What Are AI Agents?

Understanding the power of intelligent automation for your business

Intelligent Business Assistants

AI agents are sophisticated software programs that can understand, learn, and act autonomously to complete specific business tasks. Think of them as tireless digital employees that work 24/7, handling repetitive tasks with improved accuracy to significantly increase the efficiency of your business.

Three Common Opportunities We See Most

Repetitive Low-Value Tasks

Every job involves tedious, manual tasks that take people away from more strategic work. These mindless tasks slow productivity, and make work more frustrating and less fulfilling. Summarizing meeting notes, looking for trends in spreadsheet data, generating product requirements docs, answering the same questions over and overâ€¦ are all jobs AI can take on for your teams.

Skill Bottlenecks

Work often slows when employees reach the limits of their expertise and need input from other departments or experts. This can create bottlenecks and delay progress. AI can help bridge these gaps, helping employees expand their skillsets (from data analysis and trend visualization to coding) without waiting for additional support. Expert teams get more time back from lower value cross functional tasks, and everyone else learns to communicate in their language.

Navigating Ambiguity

Knowledge work often involves ambiguity and open-ended challenges. Employees may struggle to get started or become blocked, causing projects to stall. Here AI can act as a catalyst, helping to generate ideas, analyze data, and propose next steps when the path is unclear.

Not Just Automation

AI agents that work alongside your team, maintaining ~90% automation efficiency

Human-AI Collaboration

While full automation is powerful, sometimes you need the perfect balance of AI efficiency with human oversight and decision-making. Our AI agents can be designed to work in tandem with your employees, retaining approximately 90% of automation efficiency while keeping humans in the loop for quality control and final decisions.

Example: Invoice Processing with Human Review

AI Agent Processes Invoice

The agent automatically detects new invoices and extracts all relevant data including vendor, amount, date, and line items.

Side-by-Side Review Interface

Both the original invoice and extracted data are displayed in a user-friendly web interface. The employee can quickly verify accuracy and make any necessary corrections.

One-Click Approval

After review, the employee simply clicks a button to send the verified data directly to your accounting software, completing the process in seconds.

Best of Both Worlds

AI Efficiency

  • Instant data extraction
  • 24/7 processing capability
  • Consistent accuracy
  • No manual data entry

Human Oversight

  • Quality assurance
  • Exception handling
  • Business logic decisions
  • Final approval control

This approach maintains approximately 90% of full automation efficiency while providing the security and control of human oversight when needed.

Our Process

How Agentic Solutions creates intelligent agents tailored to your business

01

Discovery & Analysis

We begin by speaking directly with your employees and observing their daily tasks and workflows. This hands-on approach allows us to identify specific areas where AI agents can be most effectively implemented to streamline operations and reduce manual workload.

02

Data Assessment

Next, we conduct a comprehensive evaluation of your business data landscape. We determine what data has been collected, how it's currently being stored, and identify gaps where additional data collection would enhance AI agent effectiveness.

03

Strategic Planning

We then collaborate with your business decision makers to present our findings and recommendations. Together, we develop a concrete plan that outlines which processes will benefit from AI agents and establish a clear roadmap for implementation.

04

Phased Implementation

Finally, we execute the plan by building and integrating the simplest agents first, ensuring quick wins and immediate value. More complex agents are then deployed progressively over the following weeks, allowing for smooth adaptation and optimization.

05

Continuous Evolution

The AI field is advancing at an unprecedented rate, meaning your agents will only get better and more capable over time. Agentic Solutions is immersed in the latest technological developments, ensuring your business benefits from cutting-edge improvements as they become available and add value to your operations.
